Presentation

Developing emotional intelligence can support people to interact with others in a more positive and harmonious manner which will lead to improved overall well-being and stronger relationship. People with high EQ are tend to build better relationship with others because they know how to communicate effectively and emphasise others to gain trust.

High EQ also helps people to resolve conflicts more constructively as they respond to different opinion professionally and foster respectful discussions. EQ also plays a vital role in leadership and teamwork by promoting collaboration, motivation and inspiration.

Most importantly, high EQ helps to look after our mental well-being as it enables us to bounce back from stressful emotions, so that we are emotionally stronger and resilient. It can be seen that developing a high EQ is beneficial to both personal growth and professional success.
