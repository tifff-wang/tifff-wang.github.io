Project Overview

Tiff's Donuts is my final group project from Dev Academy. During this project, I acted as the Product Owner with five other team members where we developed a web application where users could design and customise 3D donuts. The website includes rendering 3D models, interactions and scrolling effects using threeJS and React. During this intensive 7-day project, I had the opportunity to take the lead in introducing and implementing React Three Fiber, a technology not covered in our bootcamp. This experience was truly valuable as it showcased our team's dedication to collaborative problem-solving and our ability to adapt and learn on the fly.

Tech Stacks

React Three Fiber(Drei) | React (Vite) | TailwindCSS | Auth0
React Router | SuperAgent | Vitest | PlayWright

Future possiblities

I will continue to explore different visual effects that React Three Fiber can create by adding more sections to the homepage. These sections will feature various scrolling effects, including animations of the donut from different positions and camera angles.
